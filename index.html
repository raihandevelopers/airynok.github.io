How to use when online shopping

AIrynok 101

After you have created the bookmark, you can start online shopping at any moment.

When you have found a product that you like you only need to click on the bookmark after which the AIrynok will be loaded and ready for search.

Click on the search button at AIrynok and discover suggested similar product offers from multiple marketplaces and online shops.
